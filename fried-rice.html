Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a traditional Chinese preparation of cooked rice, vegetables, protein, soy sauce, and aromatics. The ingredients are stir-fried in a large pan or wok for even flavor distribution.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Frozen Vegetable Packet or Variety Vegetable Pack

Steps

  1. Defrost the Frozen Veggies if frozen
  2. Add oil and wait for it to be hot, then add scrambled eggs and cooked rice
  3. Add Green onions and vegetables. Stir until mostly done
  4. Add soy sauce to taste
  5. Serve Well
