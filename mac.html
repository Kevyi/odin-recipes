Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a rich and creamy dish consisting of macaroni pasta mixed with a cheesy sauce. It's particularly popular in the United States.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flower
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Boiled Macaroni Pasta until adente
  2. Melt butter over a medium heat saucepan
  3. Add salt, pepper, and flower. Slowly ix until smooth
  4. Slowly add warm milk while consistently stirring
  5. Slowly Grate cheese
  6. Add macaroni and cook until done.
