Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Your Choice of Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Cooked Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Place Meat Sauce
  2. Place Lasagna Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce (Meat and Tomatoes)
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining Parmesan
